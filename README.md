# Enterprise GRC Transformation – Fintech Case Study

## Executive Summary

This repository presents a simulated end-to-end Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) transformation for a mid-sized fintech organization.

The case study models a structured consulting engagement covering enterprise risk assessment, ISO 27001:2022 implementation, internal audit preparation, and SOC 2 readiness alignment.

The objective was to transition the organization from an informal security posture to a structured, audit-ready compliance framework aligned with international standards.

## Organizational Context

Industry: Financial Technology (Fintech)  
Employees: 150  
Infrastructure: AWS Cloud + Microsoft 365  
Data Processed: Customer PII, payment tokens  
Business Drivers: Enterprise client onboarding, regulatory expectations, investor assurance  

## Engagement Objectives

1. Establish a formal enterprise risk management process  
2. Implement ISO 27001:2022 Information Security Management System (ISMS)  
3. Achieve internal audit readiness prior to certification  
4. Extend governance framework to support SOC 2 compliance  

## Methodology

The engagement was structured across four controlled phases:

### Phase 1 – Enterprise Risk Assessment
- Asset identification and classification  
- Risk assessment methodology definition  
- Enterprise risk register development  
- Risk treatment planning  

### Phase 2 – ISO 27001 ISMS Implementation
- Scope and context definition  
- Information security policy framework  
- Statement of Applicability (SoA) development  
- Control implementation planning aligned with identified risks  

### Phase 3 – Internal Audit & Remediation
- Internal audit execution  
- Identification of control deficiencies  
- Corrective action tracking  
- Residual risk reassessment  

### Phase 4 – SOC 2 Readiness Alignment
- ISO 27001 to SOC 2 control mapping  
- Gap assessment against Trust Services Criteria  
- Control narrative development  
- Evidence readiness preparation  

## Key Outcomes

• Enterprise risk visibility established  
• Governance framework formalized and documented  
• Audit findings identified and remediated  
• Multi-framework compliance readiness achieved  

## Purpose of This Portfolio

This portfolio demonstrates practical, enterprise-grade GRC implementation capability aligned with consulting and Big 4 standards.

It is designed to showcase structured risk management, control alignment, audit preparation, and compliance lifecycle thinking.
